Xinyu Shi publications news CV

Hi! I am Xinyu Shi. My research interests include Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), Visualization and Computer Vision. I long for developing innovative, intuitive and pragmatic techniques to help people better understand this data-driven world and interact with it more efficiently. I am passionate about gesture interaction, wearable techniques, VR/AR, technology-assisted learning, visualization, etc.

I am currently a research intern working at Vision Computing Lab, Alibaba DAMO Academy since July 2020. I received my B.Eng degree in Software Engineering from Xiamen University, China in 2020. I visited Dartmouth College as a research intern working on HCI projects from September 2019 to April 2020.

Contact: xinyushi0225@gmail.com
